Eridian Echo Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 15, 2026

Eridian Echo is an audio-transcription service available through diederich.ai. This policy explains what information Eridian Echo collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

Guest sessions

When you first use Eridian Echo, the service creates a random guest identifier and stores it in a long-lived browser cookie. This identifier associates your browser with your transcription history.

The cookie does not contain your name, email address, or transcript contents. If you delete or lose the cookie before connecting the session to a Google Account, we may be unable to restore access to that guest transcription history.

Google sign-in

If you choose to sign in with Google, Eridian Echo receives limited account information authorized by you, including:

  • A Google account identifier
  • Your email address
  • Your display name, when provided by Google

This information is used only to identify your Eridian Echo account, display your name, connect an existing guest session to that account, and restore your transcription history when you return.

Eridian Echo does not request access to your Gmail, Google Drive, contacts, calendar, or other Google content.

Eridian Echo’s use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.

Audio and transcription information

When you submit an audio file, Eridian Echo may store:

  • The uploaded filename
  • Processing status and timestamps
  • The resulting transcript
  • Error information if transcription fails

The audio file is temporarily stored while it is processed and is sent to Google’s Gemini service to generate the transcript. Eridian Echo attempts to delete both its temporary local copy and the remotely uploaded Gemini copy after processing completes.

Transcripts and associated job records are retained so they remain available during future visits.

Technical information

Our servers and network infrastructure may automatically record limited technical information such as your IP address, browser type, requested URL, timestamps, and application errors. This information is used to operate, troubleshoot, and secure the service.

How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide audio transcription
  • Maintain guest and authenticated sessions
  • Associate transcriptions with the correct user
  • Restore transcription history
  • Operate, diagnose, and secure the service
  • Prevent misuse

We do not sell personal information or use it for targeted advertising.

Service providers

Eridian Echo uses Google services for Google authentication, Gemini audio processing, and web fonts. Google may receive information necessary to provide those services under its applicable privacy policies and service terms.

Infrastructure providers may also process limited information as necessary to host, operate, back up, monitor, or secure the service.

We do not disclose your information to other parties except when necessary to operate the service, when required by law, or when reasonably necessary to protect the service, its operator, or its users.

Cookies

Eridian Echo uses an essential cookie to maintain your session and associate it with your transcription history.

You can delete this cookie through your browser. Deleting an unauthenticated guest cookie may permanently remove your ability to access the transcription history associated with it. If your session is connected to a Google Account, you can sign in again to restore access.

Retention and deletion

Uploaded audio is intended to be deleted after processing. Transcripts, account records, session records, and related metadata may be retained until:

  • You request their deletion;
  • They are no longer needed to provide the service; or
  • The service is discontinued.

To request access to or deletion of your information, contact diederich@gmail.com. We may require reasonable verification that you control the relevant account or guest session.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. No online service or storage system can guarantee absolute security.

Children

Eridian Echo is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the service changes. The revised policy will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
